logistics
The process of planning, coordinating and executing the distribution of people, plant and materials to safely facilitate successful delivery of our projects.
planning
Identifying the steps required to build a project. This involves defining the activities through the project lifecycle, creating an ordered sequence and identifying the people, plant and materials required.
engineering
Plan, design, monitor and inspect the delivery of our projects. This involves many different aspects such as; innovation and buildability, design and technical development, contributing to safe methodology, monitoring works, maintaining records.
design
Process of managing the description of our Projects represented by drawings and specifications in conjunction with the Design Team.
digital
Using digital tools across the management, delivery and operation of construction projects to bring about more collaborative and efficient ways of working
inclusion, wellbeing & sustainability
Our approach to Safety through inclusion and wellbeing, set out through three cores; Engineered Safety, Delivery Excellence and Human Performance. Sustainability capabilities aligned with our corporate goals and objectives.
commercial
The application of skills to maximise business potential by ensuring the successful management of the project's finances, procurement and the processes that can impact upon a project's profits, timescales and quality.
procurement
Involves the process of acquiring goods and services needed for a construction project. It usually begins with the identification of need, followed by the selection of contractors or suppliers (also known as vendors), negotiation of prices, and finally, the awarding of contracts.
Legal
Covers the legislation relevant to our industry by which we must comply when we go to work.
Project Management / Delivery
The direction, regulation, and supervision of our projects from early development to completion. The ultimate goal of construction project management is the full satisfaction of the client's demands for a viable project both in terms of functionality and budget.
